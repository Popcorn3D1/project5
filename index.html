obviously i can't use album art so i will use representational images instead.

SUPER JUNIOR / Twins (Knock Out)

A remake of English boy band Triple 8's 2003 debut single Knockout. Super Junior (then known as Super Junior 05) debuted with their version of the song in 2005. There is also a version by Taiwanese boy band Energy, 退魔录, also released in 2003.

Titanic: The Legend Goes On / Party Time

This is art. From the Italian animated film Titanic: The Legend Goes On which came out in the year 2000. This is the version of the song from the cut, dubbed version released in the US. It is extremely jarring, although the uncut and Italian versions don't make much more sense.

Reol / No Dazzle, No Break

Released with the Honkai: Star Rail Rappa Trailer, this song exemplifies the neon chaos of the animation.

原因は自分にある。 / Infinite Cynicism (無限シニシズム)

Also known as GENJIBU, their name translates to "The Cause Lies Within Me."
